Jobs for Graduate Persons

If you're a graduate and looking for government jobs in Pakistan, there are numerous opportunities available across various sectors. Here are some common job categories for graduates in Pakistan:

1. Civil Services:

CSS (Central Superior Services):

This is a competitive exam conducted by the Federal Public Service Commission (FPSC) for recruitment to the civil services of Pakistan. Successful candidates can be placed in various government departments.

2. Teaching and Education:

Lecturer/Assistant Professor:

Graduates can apply for teaching positions in colleges and universities. The hiring process is usually conducted by the respective educational institutions.

3. Banking and Finance:

Probationary Officer:

>Many banks in Pakistan conduct recruitment drives for probationary officers who go through rigorous training programs.

4. Healthcare and Medical:

Medical Officer/Medical Superintendent:

>Graduates in medical fields can apply for various positions in government hospitals and healthcare facilities.

5. Engineering and Technical:

Engineer/Technical Officer:

Graduates in engineering fields can apply for positions in government engineering organizations and departments.

6. Information Technology:

IT Officer/Software Developer:

There are IT-related positions available in various government departments and organizations.

7. Law and Judiciary:

Assistant Advocate General/Assistant Attorney:

Graduates in law can apply for legal positions in government law departments and agencies.

8. Public Administration:

Assistant Director/Public Relations Officer:

Graduates in public administration can apply for administrative positions in government departments.

9. Police and Law Enforcement:

ASP (Assistant Superintendent of Police):

Graduates can apply for higher-level positions in the police department.

10. Armed Forces and Paramilitary Jobs:

Army/Navy/Air Force Officer:

Graduates can join the armed forces through various recruitment programs.

11. Foreign Service:

Foreign Service Officer:

This involves representing Pakistan in diplomatic missions abroad. The recruitment process is conducted by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

12. National Testing Services (NTS) and Pakistan Testing Services (PTS):

These agencies conduct recruitment tests for various government departments. Graduates can apply for positions advertised through these testing services.
