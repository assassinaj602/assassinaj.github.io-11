Pakistan Tourism Development Corporation (PTDC)

The Pakistan Tourism Development Corporation (PTDC) is a government-owned organization responsible for promoting and developing tourism in Pakistan. Here is some information about PTDC:

1. Establishment and Purpose:

PTDC was established in 1970 by the Government of Pakistan with the goal of promoting tourism in the country. It operates under the administrative control of the Ministry of Tourism.

2. Functions:

PTDC's primary functions include:
       1. Developing and promoting tourist destinations in Pakistan.
       2. Managing and operating tourist facilities and accommodations.
       3. Arranging tours and providing tourist services.
       4.Conducting marketing and promotional activities to attract domestic and international tourists.
       5.Collaborating with government and private sector stakeholders in the tourism industry.

3. Tourist Facilities:

PTDC operates a network of tourist facilities across Pakistan, including motels, rest houses, and resorts. These facilities are located in popular tourist destinations and provide accommodation options for travelers.

4. Tour Packages and Services:

PTDC offers a range of tour packages and services for both domestic and international tourists. These may include guided tours, transportation services, and assistance with travel arrangements.

5. Promotion of Tourism:

PTDC is involved in various promotional activities to boost tourism in Pakistan. This includes participating in travel fairs, organizing cultural events, and running marketing campaigns. Collaborations and Partnerships: PTDC works in collaboration with other government departments, provincial tourism authorities, and private sector organizations to achieve its objectives.

7. Online Presence:

PTDC maintains an online presence through its official website, where tourists can find information about tourist destinations, facilities, and services offered.
